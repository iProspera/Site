Long-Term Care Coverage

At Prospera, we recognize that planning for long-term care is one of the most important — and often overlooked — aspects of a comprehensive financial strategy. The reality is that most Americans will need some form of extended care during their lifetime, whether at home, in an assisted living facility, or in a nursing home. Without a plan in place, the costs of long-term care can quickly erode the savings and assets you've worked a lifetime to build.

Our approach to long-term care coverage is built around understanding your unique situation, family dynamics, and financial goals. We help clients evaluate their risk exposure, compare traditional long-term care insurance with hybrid alternatives that combine coverage with life insurance or annuities, and identify the right strategy to protect both your independence and your legacy. With access to a broad network of carriers, we ensure you see a full range of options — so you can make a confident, informed decision.

Whether you're just beginning to think about long-term care or ready to put a plan in motion, Prospera is here to guide you through every step of the process.

Business Insurance Solutions

Our team works with multiple carriers to find the right coverage mix for your specific industry, size, and risk profile.
